# Hook Options

## Option 1: Bold Claim + Curiosity

Imagine doubling your client base without lifting a finger. How does Example Agency's secret formula make it possible for businesses like yours?

**Why this works:** This hook makes a bold claim about achieving a highly desirable outcome—doubling the client base—while creating a curiosity gap by hinting at a 'secret formula' that prompts the viewer to want to learn more.

---

## Option 2: Pattern Interrupt + Question

Tired of chasing leads with no results? What if the leads came to you instead?

**Why this works:** The hook begins with a statement that resonates with the target audience's frustration, immediately capturing attention. The subsequent question interrupts the typical thought pattern and suggests an intriguing alternative, encouraging further engagement.

---

## Option 3: Social Proof + Promise

Join the ranks of savvy entrepreneurs who’ve transformed their businesses with our lead generation magic. Are you ready for a steady stream of leads?

**Why this works:** This hook leverages social proof by implying that successful, savvy entrepreneurs are already using the service, which suggests credibility and effectiveness. The promise of a 'steady stream of leads' is a powerful motivator for the target audience.
