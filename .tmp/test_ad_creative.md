## Additional Testing Recommendations

**Cross-Ad Variables to Test:**
- CTA buttons: "Learn More" vs. "Get Started" vs. "Apply Now"
- Number specificity: "3x revenue" vs. "triple revenue"
- Timeframes: "6 months" vs. "this year"

**Winning Elements to Scale:**
- Best-performing headline across all audiences
- Top-converting visual format (static vs. carousel vs. video)
- Most effective pain point or benefit message

**Budget Allocation Suggestion:**
- 40% to pain point ad (broader appeal)
- 35% to benefit/outcome ad (high-intent audience)
- 25% to social proof ad (warm audiences/retargeting)
