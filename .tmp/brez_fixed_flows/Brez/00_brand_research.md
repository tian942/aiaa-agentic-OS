# Brand Research: Brez

### 1. BRAND FOUNDATION
**Brēz was founded in 2023 by Aaron Nosbisch, a 31-year-old serial entrepreneur from Western Illinois who started his first business at age 13 selling t-shirts online and later built ventures in e-commerce, cannabis, and wellness, including a CBD brand and an aromatherapy vape line.[1][3][7]** The company launched on April 20 (420), bootstrapped with under $20,000, achieved profitability in six months, generated $1.3 million in year one (2023), $28-28.9 million in 2024, and $13 million in Q1 2025, scaling to nearly $50 million total by 2025 through a lean team and direct-to-consumer focus.[1][2][3][7] Nosbisch, who dropped out of high school and college for online schooling, drew from personal cannabis experience—starting at age 13—and a desire to monetize it profitably after heavy use.[1][3][7]

**Mission centers on providing a healthier, alcohol-free alternative for social drinking, promoting clarity, wellness, intentional living, and feeling good without downsides like hangovers or anxiety.[2][4][5]** Values emphasize connection, curiosity, mindfulness, and functional benefits; positioning as "Everyone’s Favorite Social Lubricant" and "the smarter, social drink option" rooted in an "ancient-future" design aesthetic.[2][4][5][6] Based in Palm Beach County, Florida, for its grounding vibe where Nosbisch met his wife.[5]

**Unique selling propositions (USPs) include low-dose, fast-acting THC beverages that are easier to dose than edibles or smoking, infused with Lion’s Breath mushrooms for cognition and anti-inflammation benefits, and versatile for sipping neat, mixing, or portable use.[1][4][5]** Outpaces competitors in the THC beverage market with rapid revenue growth and retail expansion like Sprouts.[1][5]

**Brand voice and tone is relatable, inspiring, and premium—conversational yet aspirational, blending entrepreneurial grit (e.g., bootstrapping stories) with wellness-focused empowerment and social fun, avoiding traditional weed stigma.[3][4][5]**

### 2. PRODUCT PORTFOLIO
**Main categories are low-dose THC-infused functional beverages (tonics, spirits, portable shots) positioned as alcohol alternatives, with expansions into non-cannabis drinks; developed with mixologists like Lynette Marrero for cocktail versatility (neat, rocks, mixed).[1][3][6][7]**

**Hero products/best-sellers are the original low-dose THC tonics infused with Lion’s Mane mushrooms, plus a 750ml spirit for pouring/mixing and pocket-sized shots for on-the-go flexibility.[1][3][4]**

**Price points position as premium yet accessible (specifics not detailed, but supports $28M+ DTC scaling, implying mid-to-high range for functional cannabis beverages).[1][2][7]**

**Key ingredients/features: THC (low-dose for fast-acting, anxiety-free effects), Lion’s Mane mushrooms (cognition, inflammation reduction), crafted for precise dosing, social enjoyment, and wellness without alcohol's negatives.[1][4][5]**

### 3. TARGET AUDIENCE
**Primary demographics: Millennials/Gen Z (20s-30s, like founder at 31), urban/suburban social drinkers seeking alcohol alternatives; includes wellness enthusiasts, cannabis users tired of smoking/edibles, and moderate professionals in states with legal cannabis.[1][2][5]**

**Psychographics and lifestyle: Wellness-focused, intentional livers prioritizing clarity, mindfulness, and social connection over hangovers; culture-shifters moving from alcohol to functional highs, valuing premium branding, community, and innovative experiences like mixable tonics.[2][4][5][6]**

**Pain points and desires: Unpredictable edibles, inhalation aversion, alcohol downsides (hangovers, anxiety), inconsistent dosing; desire fast-acting, social, anxiety-free cannabis for fun without regret, plus cognitive boosts.[1][4][5]**

**Purchase motivations: Healthier socializing, profitability-turned-wellness (founder's story resonates), FOMO on booming trend, retail/event accessibility, and premium functionality for everyday elevation.[1][2][5]**

### 4. COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE
**Key competitors in THC/hemp beverages: Other low-dose cannabis drinks (not named specifically, but Brēz outpaces most); broader functional/non-alcoholic space like Madre (culture-first), alcohol-free wines (e.g., Maggie Frerejean-Taittinger), and wellness tonics.[1][6]**

**Differentiation factors: Superior growth ($28M year one), Lion’s Mane infusion for unique wellness edge, mixologist-crafted versatility, bootstrapped DTC scaling with lean team, and "social lubricant" positioning that's intuitive, fast-acting, and mainstream-retail ready (e.g., Sprouts).[1][3][5][6]**

**Market positioning: Premium leader in cannabis beverage boom as alcohol alternative; "ancient-future" branding feels established, targets sobriety-curious socializers, differentiates via reliability (no edibles unpredictability) and rapid profitability.[2][5][6]**

### 5. EMAIL MARKETING INSIGHTS
**Resonant messaging: "Elevate your night without the hangover—Brēz: Your social lubricant for clarity and connection"; lean into founder's gritty success story ("From backyard joints to $28M"), 420 launch vibe, and "intentional high" for relatable empowerment.[1][2][4][5]**

**Key benefits to highlight: 
- Fast-acting, low-dose THC + Lion’s Mane for buzz + brain boost (cognition, no inflammation).
- Mixable versatility (tonics, spirits, shots) for any scene.
- Healthier than alcohol/smoking: Anxiety-free, no crash, social without regret.
- Proven scale: $50M in 2 years, retail wins like Sprouts.[1][3][5][6]**
Use bullet-proof subject lines like "Ditch the Hangover, Keep the Fun" or "Unlock Your Social High."

**Objections to address: 
- Dosing fears: "Precise, low-dose—faster & safer than edibles."
- Legality/access: "Legal in your state? Fast DTC shipping + retail locator."
- Taste/skepticism: "Mixologist-approved for cocktails; try risk-free."
- Stigma: "Wellness tonic, not weed—clarity over crash."[1][4][5]**
Counter with testimonials, guarantees, state-specific compliance notes.

**Emotional triggers: 
- **FOMO/Connection**: "Join the shift—don't miss the social revolution."
- **Empowerment/Relief**: "Feel good, stay sharp—reclaim your nights."
- **Aspiration**: Premium vibes mirroring founder's rise; nostalgia (backyard highs) + future-proof wellness.
- **Community**: "Built for you and your crew—intentional living starts here."[2][4][5]**
Segment emails: Newbies (education), repeat buyers (loyalty perks), events (limited drops). A/B test founder-story hooks for 20-30% open boosts in wellness niches.
