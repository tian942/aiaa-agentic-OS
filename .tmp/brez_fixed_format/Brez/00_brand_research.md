# Brand Research: Brez

### 1. BRAND FOUNDATION
**Brez was founded in 2023 by Aaron Nosbisch, a 31-year-old serial entrepreneur from Western Illinois who started his first business at age 13 selling t-shirts online and later built ventures in e-commerce, cannabis, and wellness, including a CBD brand and an aromatherapy vape line.**[1][3][7] Nosbisch bootstrapped Brez with under $20,000, launching on 4/20, drawing from his experience to assemble a lean team of experts; the brand achieved profitability in 6 months, $1.3M revenue in year one (2023), $28M in 2024, and $13M in Q1 2025, outpacing THC beverage competitors.[1][2][3][7] Based in Palm Beach County, Florida—where Nosbisch met his wife—it emphasizes a "grounding and magical" environment for mindful growth.[5]

**Mission centers on redefining social drinking as a healthier, alcohol-free alternative that promotes clarity, wellness, intentional living, and connection without downsides like hangovers or anxiety.**[2][4][5] Values include innovation in functional beverages, community-building, and overcoming cannabis marketing hurdles; positioning as "Everyone’s Favorite Social Lubricant" fills the gap for fast-acting, easy-to-dose cannabis drinks superior to unpredictable edibles or inhalation.[4][5]

**Unique selling propositions (USPs) are low-dose THC beverages infused with Lion’s Mane mushrooms for cognitive and anti-inflammatory benefits, precise dosing for anxiety-free effects, and versatility (e.g., tonics, spirits, portable shots for mixing).** [1][3][4]

**Brand voice and tone are approachable, inspiring, and culturally resonant—relatable storytelling from Nosbisch's self-made journey (high school/college dropout), with an "ancient-future" aesthetic blending timeless design, premium feel, and modern wellness vibes for social, feel-good experiences.**[2][5][6]

### 2. PRODUCT PORTFOLIO
**Main categories include low-dose THC-infused functional beverages (tonics, spirits in 750ml bottles for mixing/sipping, and portable shots), with expansions into non-cannabis drinks; crafted with input from hundreds of bartenders (e.g., mixology legend Lynette Marrero) for cocktail versatility—neat, on rocks, or mixed.**[1][3][4][6]

**Best-sellers and hero products are the original Lion’s Mane-infused THC tonics, driving rapid scaling to $28M+ revenue as America's top-selling cannabis drink.**[1][2]

**Price points position as premium (not specified numerically in sources, but supported by high revenue and retail partnerships like Sprouts), targeting aspirational wellness consumers seeking alcohol alternatives.**[2][5]

**Key ingredients/features: Low-dose THC for fast-acting, predictable effects; Lion’s Mane mushrooms for cognition/inflammation benefits; functional formulation emphasizing no hangover, clarity, and social enjoyment over alcohol's negatives.**[1][4]

### 3. TARGET AUDIENCE
**Primary demographics: Millennials/Gen Z (aligned with Nosbisch's age 31 and Gen Z trends), wellness-focused adults in the US, especially in cannabis-legal states; direct-to-consumer buyers and retail shoppers at places like Sprouts; social drinkers aged 21-40 seeking alcohol alternatives.**[2][5][6]

**Psychographics and lifestyle: Intentional livers prioritizing clarity, wellness, and mindful socializing; reject alcohol's downsides (hangovers, poor cognition); embrace functional, non-psychoactive highs; community-oriented, culture-driven (e.g., events, TikTok virality), with curiosity for innovative cannabis formats.[2][4][5][6]

**Pain points and desires: Unpredictable edibles/inhalation, alcohol's health tolls (inflammation, anxiety, regret); desire fast, dosable, social highs that enhance connection/cognition without crashes; seek premium, intuitive products for everyday rituals or nights out.**[1][5]

**Purchase motivations: Healthier social lubricant for feel-good vibes; FOMO on booming non-alc trend; trust in founder-led innovation; convenience for mixing/portability; mainstream accessibility via DTC and retail.**[4][5]

### 4. COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE
**Key competitors in THC/CBD functional beverages include other low-dose cannabis drinks (outpaced by Brez per revenue metrics), plus non-cannabis alcohol alternatives like moderation wines (e.g., Maggie Frerejean-Taittinger's 0.0% sparkling) and culture-first brands like Madre (Gen Z/mom-focused virals).**[1][2][6]

| Aspect              | Brez Differentiation                                                                 | Competitor Examples                          |
|---------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------|
| **Formulation**    | Low-dose THC + Lion’s Mane for precise, cognitive-boosting effects; bartender-tested mixability | Edibles/inhalation (unpredictable); NA wines (no cannabis) [1][5][6] |
| **Performance**    | Fast-acting, anxiety-free social high; versatile formats (shots/spirits)             | Slower onset or less social formats [3][5]   |
| **Scaling/Success**| $28M year one, bootstrapped profitability; DTC + retail (Sprouts)                    | Slower growth, higher capital needs [2][7]   |
| **Branding**       | Self-made founder story, "ancient-future" premium aesthetic                          | Culture/community focus (e.g., Madre pre-launch IG) [6] |

**Market positioning: Premium leader in THC beverages as the intuitive, superior alcohol swap—faster growth via e-commerce expertise, lean ops, and wellness edge; mainstreaming via retail while staying DTC-agile.**[1][2][5]

### 5. EMAIL MARKETING INSIGHTS
**Resonant messaging: "Elevate your nights without the crash—Brez: Clarity in every sip" or founder-led stories like "From backyard joints to $28M: Join the social revolution"; emphasize "social lubricant" for effortless vibes, 4/20 launch lore, and rapid success proof.**[1][4][5]

**Key benefits to highlight: 
- Predictable, low-dose bliss with Lion’s Mane brain boost—no anxiety, hangover, or fog.
- Mix-anywhere versatility for real-life socializing (home, bar, events).
- Proven scale: #1 seller, $13M Q1 2025—your guilt-free upgrade to alcohol.
- Wellness wins: Cognition, inflammation relief, intentional highs.**[1][2][3][4]

**Objections to address: 
- Dosing fears: "Fast-acting, easy-control—better than edibles."
- Legality/access: "DTC shipped to legal states; now in Sprouts."
- Taste/skepticism: "Bartender-approved for cocktails; try risk-free."
- Premium price: "Invest in clarity—outperforms booze long-term."** [3][5]

**Emotional triggers: 
- **FOMO/Community**: "Don't miss the shift—join 50M+ ditching alcohol."
- **Empowerment**: Nosbisch's dropout-to-CEO grit inspires self-made wins.
- **Relief/Aspiration**: Trade regret for clarity; "Feel good, connect deeper."
- **Exclusivity**: Early adopter status in explosive category.** [2][4][6]
