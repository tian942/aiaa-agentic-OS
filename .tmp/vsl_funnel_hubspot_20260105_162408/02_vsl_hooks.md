# Hook Options

## Option 1: Bold Claim + Curiosity

Unlock a 300% increase in leads with HubSpot's Marketing Hub—find out how top brands like Kelly Services did it!

**Why this works:** This hook makes a bold claim about lead increase, which immediately grabs attention. Mentioning well-known brands like Kelly Services adds credibility, while the promise of revealing how they achieved these results creates a curiosity gap.

---

## Option 2: Pattern Interrupt + Question

Tired of stagnant growth? What if your marketing could deliver 10x the results overnight?

**Why this works:** The opening question disrupts the viewer's pattern by addressing a common frustration (stagnant growth) and then poses a provocative question that challenges their expectations, prompting them to seek answers.

---

## Option 3: Social Proof + Promise

Join companies like Reed and Datanyze in skyrocketing your traffic—guaranteed!

**Why this works:** This hook uses social proof by referencing successful companies, creating an instant association with success. The word 'guaranteed' promises results, making it hard for the audience to ignore.
