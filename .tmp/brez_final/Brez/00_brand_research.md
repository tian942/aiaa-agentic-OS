# Brand Research: Brez

### 1. BRAND FOUNDATION
**BRĒZ was founded in 2023 by Aaron Nosbisch, a 31-year-old serial entrepreneur from Western Illinois who started his first business at age 13 selling t-shirts online and later built ventures in e-commerce, cannabis, and wellness, including a CBD brand and an aromatherapy vape line.**[1][3][8] Nosbisch launched BRĒZ on April 20 (4/20) after identifying a gap for fast-acting, easy-to-dose cannabis beverages as a social alternative to alcohol, drawing from his personal experiences with cannabis and a desire to innovate social drinking without traditional downsides like unpredictability of edibles or inhalation.[1][5][7] The company achieved $1.3 million in first-year revenue, $28 million in year two, and $13 million in Q1 2025, reaching near $50 million total while bootstrapping with a lean team.[1][2][3]

**Mission centers on providing clarity, wellness, intentional living, and mindful consumption through functional, non-alcoholic social tonics that foster connection without alcohol's hangovers or cannabis's common pitfalls.**[2][4][5][7] Values emphasize balance, innovation, organic federally legal hemp extracts (per 2018 Farm Bill), and community, with Nosbisch basing operations in Palm Beach County, Florida, for its grounding vibe.[5][7]

**Positioning as "Everyone’s Favorite Social Lubricant" and "healthier alternative to social drinking" targets the shift from alcohol to functional beverages.[4][5]

**Unique selling propositions (USPs) include low-dose, full-spectrum THC/CBD with Lion’s Mane mushrooms for cognition and anti-inflammation benefits, fast onset, precise dosing, and versatility (cans, spirits, portable shots).[1][3][7]

**Brand voice and tone is approachable, inspiring, and relatable—blending entrepreneurial grit (dropout story), wellness focus, and social fun with an "ancient-future" aesthetic for premium, mindful vibes.[2][3][6]

### 2. PRODUCT PORTFOLIO
**Main categories are low-dose THC/CBD functional social tonics: initial cans, expanded to 750ml spirits for mixing/sipping, and portable shots for on-the-go use at events or restaurants.**[1][3][7]

**Best-sellers and hero products are the core THC-infused cans with Lion’s Mane, driving rapid scaling as America's top-selling cannabis drink; spirits and shots build on this as flexible extensions.[1][2][3]

**Price points position as premium (specifics not detailed, but aligned with functional beverage market for retail like Sprouts), emphasizing value through science-backed efficacy over cheap alternatives.[5][6]

**Key ingredients/features: full-spectrum hemp cannabinoids (federally legal), organic extracts, Lion’s Mane mushrooms for cognitive/inflammation benefits; low-dose for anxiety-free, fast-acting social buzz without alcohol downsides.[1][7]

### 3. TARGET AUDIENCE
**Primary demographics: Millennials/Gen Z (20s-30s like founder), urban/suburban wellness enthusiasts in U.S. markets with legal cannabis access, including social drinkers seeking alternatives.[2][5]

**Psychographics and lifestyle: Intentional livers prioritizing clarity, balance, mindfulness, and non-alcoholic socializing; cannabis-curious but dosage-averse to smoking/edibles; value wellness, community, and premium functional products.[2][5][7]

**Pain points and desires: Alcohol hangovers, unpredictable edibles/inhalation, lack of social cannabis options; desire fast, reliable buzz for unwinding, events, or mixing without regret.[1][5][7]

**Purchase motivations: Healthier social lubricant for presence and connection, precise dosing for confidence, innovative flavors/versatility as alcohol replacement in a growing sober-curious trend.[4][5][7]

### 4. COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE
**Key competitors: Other THC beverages (outpacing most), non-alcoholic tonics like OLIPOP (functional sodas), alcohol-free wines/spirits (e.g., moderation brands), and cannabis edibles/vapes.[1][2][6]

| Aspect | BRĒZ Differentiation | Competitor Examples |
|--------|----------------------|---------------------|
| **Formulation** | Low-dose THC/CBD + Lion’s Mane for fast, clear-headed buzz | OLIPOP: Prebiotics/glucose stability; edibles: Unpredictable[1][6] |
| **Social Fit** | Mixable spirits/shots as intuitive alcohol swap | Non-alc wines: Ritual-only; vapes: Inhalation barrier[3][5] |
| **Growth/Scaling** | $28M year one via e-com/wellness expertise, lean team | Slower retail ramps for others[1][2][3] |
| **Branding** | "Social lubricant" for mindful fun | Nostalgia (OLIPOP) or luxury (wines)[5][6] |

**Market positioning: Premium leader in THC functional beverages, bridging cannabis/wellness with mainstream retail (e.g., Sprouts), ahead via speed-to-market and consumer-friendly format.[1][5]

### 5. EMAIL MARKETING INSIGHTS
**Resonant messaging: "Elevate your nights without the crash—BRĒZ: Clarity in every sip" or "Ditch the hangover, keep the vibe," tying to social proof ($28M sales, founder story) and 4/20 launch heritage for cannabis fans.[1][4][5]

**Key benefits to highlight: 
- Fast-acting, precise low-dose buzz for social confidence.
- Lion’s Mane-powered focus/no inflammation vs. alcohol.
- Versatile (cans/spirits/shots) for any scene.
- Organic, legal hemp for guilt-free wellness.[1][3][7]

**Objections to address: 
- Dosing fears: "Anxiety-free, easier than edibles."
- Legality/access: "Federally legal hemp—shop nationwide."
- Taste/onset: "Mixes like spirits, hits fast."
- Novelty skepticism: "Top-selling for a reason—$50M proof."[1][5][7]

**Emotional triggers: 
- **Nostalgia/relief**: Swap boozy regrets for clear mornings.
- **Belonging**: "Join the sober-curious crew unwinding better."
- **Empowerment**: Intentional choices for presence and connection.
- **Aspirational fun**: Founder’s hustle story + "social lubricant" vibe for FOMO-free nights.[2][4][5]
