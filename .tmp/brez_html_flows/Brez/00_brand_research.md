# Brez Email Marketing Research

## 1. BRAND FOUNDATION

**Company Background & Founding Story**

Brez was **founded in 2023 by Aaron Nosbisch**, a serial entrepreneur who started his first business at age 13[3]. Nosbisch built his expertise across e-commerce, cannabis, and wellness before launching Brez on April 20, 2023 (420, cannabis culture's high holiday)[1]. The company achieved remarkable early traction: $1.3 million in revenue during its first year, becoming profitable within 6 months[1]. By 2024, Brez scaled to $28.9 million in annual revenue, with $13 million generated in Q1 2025[1][7].

**Mission, Values & Positioning**

Brez positions itself as **an alcohol alternative brand** centered on clarity, wellness, and intentional living[2]. The brand addresses a cultural shift where consumers are moving away from alcohol toward functional beverages that support mental clarity and social connection without negative consequences[2][4]. Nosbisch emphasizes that the brand is rooted in "connection, curiosity, and clarity"[5].

**Unique Selling Propositions**

- **Functional ingredient focus**: Infused with Lion's Mane mushrooms, believed to support cognition and reduce inflammation[1]
- **Fast-acting, predictable dosing**: Beverages offer faster onset and more consistent effects than edibles[5]
- **Social-first design**: Crafted specifically for social occasions as an alcohol replacement[5]
- **Multiple consumption formats**: Available as ready-to-drink beverages, spirits (750ml bottles for mixing), and portable shot versions[3]
- **Premium positioning**: Developed with bartenders and mixology experts to perform in classic cocktails[6]

**Brand Voice & Tone**

Brez communicates with sophistication and intentionality. The brand emphasizes **feeling good without downsides**, positioning cannabis consumption as a mindful, wellness-oriented choice rather than recreational indulgence[4]. The aesthetic is described as "ancient-future" design—timeless and distinctive from day one[6].

## 2. PRODUCT PORTFOLIO

**Main Product Categories**

- **Ready-to-drink beverages**: Low-dose THC drinks with Lion's Mane mushrooms
- **Cannabis spirits**: 750ml bottles designed for mixing and sipping
- **Portable shots**: Pocket-sized spirit versions for on-the-go consumption[3]
- **Non-cannabis drinks**: The brand has expanded beyond THC products[7]

**Product Positioning**

Products are positioned as **functional beverages** rather than traditional cannabis products. The emphasis is on health benefits, social utility, and intentional consumption. Brez is available at major retailers including Sprouts[5], indicating mainstream distribution beyond specialty cannabis channels.

**Key Ingredients & Features**

- **Lion's Mane mushrooms**: Primary functional ingredient supporting cognition and inflammation reduction
- **Low-dose THC formulation**: Designed for anxiety-free, predictable effects
- **Bartender-crafted formulations**: Developed with mixology experts including Lynette Marrero to work in classic cocktails[6]

## 3. TARGET AUDIENCE

**Primary Demographics**

- **Age**: Primarily adults 21+ seeking alcohol alternatives
- **Lifestyle**: Health-conscious, wellness-oriented consumers
- **Geography**: Initially Palm Beach County-based, now national distribution
- **Income**: Premium positioning suggests middle to upper-middle income consumers willing to pay for quality functional beverages

**Psychographics & Lifestyle**

Brez customers are:
- **Intentional consumers** seeking clarity and mindfulness over intoxication
- **Social drinkers** who want to participate in social occasions without alcohol
- **Wellness-focused** individuals interested in functional ingredients and cognitive health
- **Culturally aware** about cannabis legalization and open to alternative consumption methods
- **Quality-conscious** and willing to invest in premium products

**Pain Points & Desires**

| Pain Point | Desire |
|---|---|
| Alcohol's negative health effects and hangovers | Feeling good without downsides |
| Unpredictable edible effects and slow onset | Fast-acting, consistent dosing |
| Social pressure to drink alcohol | Confident social participation without alcohol |
| Limited cannabis consumption formats | Intuitive, social-friendly consumption methods |
| Lack of functional beverage options | Health benefits integrated into social drinks |

**Purchase Motivations**

- Wellness and cognitive health benefits
- Social confidence and peer acceptance
- Escape from alcohol culture without social isolation
- Intentional, mindful consumption practices
- Premium quality and expert formulation

## 4. COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE

**Market Position**

Brez is **outpacing nearly every other competitor** in the THC beverage category[1]. The brand operates in the cannabis beverage sector, which Nosbisch identifies as "the cannabis industry's buzziest sector"[1].

**Differentiation Factors**

- **First-mover advantage**: Launched at the right market moment when consumer demand for alcohol alternatives was accelerating[2]
- **Functional ingredient integration**: Lion's Mane differentiation sets Brez apart from basic THC beverages
- **Bartender-crafted formulation**: Premium positioning through mixology expertise
- **Multi-format product strategy**: Offering beverages, spirits, and shots provides flexibility competitors may lack
- **Brand-first architecture**: Established premium aesthetic and tone from day one, supporting premium pricing[6]
- **Lean, expert team**: Scaled to $28M+ revenue with a small headcount by recruiting "the smartest people" from Nosbisch's network[3]

## 5. EMAIL MARKETING INSIGHTS

**Messaging That Resonates**

- **Clarity over intoxication**: "Feel good without the fog"—emphasize mental clarity as the primary benefit
- **Social confidence**: Position Brez as enabling authentic social participation without alcohol's downsides
- **Wellness narrative**: Frame as a functional beverage supporting cognitive health, not just a cannabis product
- **Intentional living**: Appeal to consumers seeking mindful, purposeful consumption
- **Expert formulation**: Highlight bartender development and Lion's Mane science to justify premium pricing

**Key Benefits to Highlight in Email**

1. **Fast-acting, predictable effects**: Unlike edibles, Brez delivers consistent results
2. **Cognitive support**: Lion's Mane mushroom benefits for focus and mental clarity
3. **Social flexibility**: Multiple formats (ready-to-drink, spirits, shots) for different occasions
4. **Premium quality**: Crafted with mixology experts for sophisticated taste
5. **Alcohol-free wellness**: Health benefits without hangovers or negative consequences
6. **Mainstream availability**: Now at retailers like Sprouts, making it accessible and legitimate

**Objections to Address**

| Objection | Email Strategy |
|---|---|
| "Cannabis is unpredictable" | Emphasize low-dose, consistent formulation and fast-acting onset vs. edibles |
| "Is this just getting high?" | Reframe as functional wellness beverage supporting cognitive health |
| "Isn't this expensive?" | Justify premium pricing through expert formulation, quality ingredients, and health benefits |
| "Will it show up in drug tests?" | Address directly if relevant to audience; position as intentional choice |
| "I don't know how to use it" | Provide clear dosing guidance and multiple consumption formats for different situations |
| "Is it legal where I am?" | Include compliance messaging and state-specific information |

**Emotional Triggers & Email Angles**

- **FOMO (Fear of Missing Out)**: "Join the movement away from alcohol" messaging; highlight rapid brand growth and mainstream adoption
- **Belonging**: Position as part of a community of intentional, wellness-focused consumers
- **Empowerment**: Frame as taking control of your social experience and health choices
- **Sophistication**: Appeal to premium positioning—"bartender-crafted," "expert-formulated"
- **Relief**: "Finally, a way to socialize without the downsides"
- **Curiosity**: Highlight functional ingredients and science-backed benefits
- **Authenticity**: Nosbisch's personal journey from cannabis user to entrepreneur creates relatable founder narrative

2. **Alcohol-alternative seekers**: Target messaging around health benefits and social confidence
3. **Wellness enthusiasts**: Emphasize Lion's Mane and cognitive health benefits
4. **Social/lifestyle segment**: Highlight multiple formats and social occasions
