# Brand Research: Brez

### 1. BRAND FOUNDATION
**BRĒZ was founded in 2023 by serial entrepreneur Aaron Nosbisch (primarily credited as founder and CEO), alongside co-founder Nick Shackelford, as a direct-to-consumer cannabis-infused beverage brand offering healthier alternatives to alcohol.**[1][2][3][5] Nosbisch, who started in business at age 13 with a t-shirt company and later built cannabis advertising and CBD ventures, identified a market gap for fast-acting, low-dose THC drinks after personal experiences with excessive cannabis use and advertising challenges in the industry.[2][4][5][6] The company launched on 4/20/2023, quickly achieving profitability within 6 months, $1.3M in year-one revenue, $28M in year two, and $13M in Q1 2025, scaling to nearly $50M total by 2025.[2][3][4]

**Mission centers on providing clarity, wellness, intentional living, and enjoyable social experiences without alcohol's downsides, positioning BRĒZ as a "next-gen social tonic" and "everyone's favorite social lubricant."**[1][3][5][6] Core values emphasize functional benefits like cognition and reduced inflammation via ingredients such as Lion’s Mane mushrooms, alongside community, curiosity, and meaningful connections; the brand is based in Palm Beach County, Florida, for its grounding vibe.[5][6]

**Unique selling propositions (USPs) include low-dose, fast-acting THC seltzers that are anxiety-free, easier to dose than edibles or inhalation, and versatile for social settings—outpacing competitors in the THC beverage sector.**[1][2][6]

**Brand voice and tone are approachable, aspirational, and wellness-focused: energetic storytelling from Nosbisch's unconventional path (high school/college dropout), with relatable vibes around "feeling good without negative consequences," nostalgia-free innovation, and premium, mindful social drinking.**[1][3][4][5][6][8]

### 2. PRODUCT PORTFOLIO
**Main categories are cannabis-infused functional seltzers/tonics (low-dose THC/CBD), expanded to spirits (750ml bottles for mixing/sipping) and portable shots for on-the-go use.**[1][2][4][5][6]

**Hero products are the original low-dose THC seltzers infused with Lion’s Mane mushrooms for cognition and anti-inflammation benefits, marketed as best-sellers driving rapid growth.**[2][5]

**Price points position as premium functional beverages (specifics not detailed in sources), emphasizing value through health and social utility over alcohol.**[3][6]

**Key ingredients/features: Low-dose THC for predictable effects, Lion’s Mane mushrooms for cognitive/inflammation support, fast-acting formula, zero alcohol, and versatility (e.g., pocket shots for restaurants).[2][4][5][6]

### 3. TARGET AUDIENCE
**Primary demographics: Millennials/Gen Z (e.g., Nosbisch at 31 reflects founder alignment), urban/suburban social drinkers aged 21-35 in legal cannabis markets, including wellness-oriented professionals.[2][3][6]

**Psychographics and lifestyle: Health-conscious "sober-curious" individuals prioritizing clarity, intentionality, and wellness over hangovers; social butterflies seeking alcohol alternatives for events, gatherings, or daily rituals; cannabis enthusiasts tired of unpredictable edibles/vaping.[1][3][5][6]

**Pain points and desires: Alcohol's negative effects (hangovers, health decline), unreliable cannabis dosing (edibles too slow/strong, smoking inconvenient), ad restrictions in cannabis marketing; desire for confident, enjoyable social highs with functional perks like focus.[2][5][6]

**Purchase motivations: Shift to non-alcoholic functionals for better mornings/socializing, proven revenue traction signaling quality, DTC accessibility, and retail expansion (e.g., Sprouts).[2][3][6]

### 4. COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE
**Key competitors: Other THC/CBD beverages (not named specifically, but BRĒZ outpaces "nearly every other" in the sector); broader functional/non-alcoholic space like OLIPOP (gut health sodas) or premium NA spirits (e.g., moderation-focused wines).[2][7]

| Aspect              | BRĒZ Differentiation                          | Competitor Context (e.g., THC Beverages/Functionals) |
|---------------------|-----------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------|
| **Growth/Speed**   | $28M in 2 years, bootstrapped, profitable fast[2][3][4] | Slower scaling, less DTC focus[2]                  |
| **Formulation**    | Low-dose THC + Lion’s Mane for cognition, fast-acting[2][5] | Unpredictable edibles/inhalation; less functional add-ins[6] |
| **Positioning**    | Social lubricant for clarity/wellness, versatile formats[1][6] | Generic cannabis or gut/NA focus without social THC edge[7] |
| **Market Edge**    | E-commerce/cannabis ad expertise, retail push[1][6] | Heavier reliance on traditional channels[7]         |

**Overall positioning: Premium leader in THC functional beverages, bridging cannabis-wellness gap as alcohol alternative, with DTC roots expanding to retail amid rising non-alcoholic demand.**[1][3][6]

### 5. EMAIL MARKETING INSIGHTS
**Resonant messaging: "Elevate your social game without the crash—clarity, connection, and good vibes in every sip," founder story hooks (e.g., "From backyard joints to $28M breakout"), limited drops tied to 4/20 or wellness events.**[1][2][5]

**Key benefits to highlight: Hangover-free highs, Lion’s Mane cognitive boost, precise low-dose control, versatile formats (seltzer/spirits/shots) for any scene; back with revenue proof ($50M scaled).[2][3][4][6]

**Objections to address: Dosing fears ("Fast-acting, anxiety-free—easier than edibles"), legality/shipping ("DTC in legal states, retail at Sprouts"), taste skepticism ("Social tonic that mixes like a pro"); use testimonials and science-backed ingredients.[5][6]

**Emotional triggers: **Nostalgia-free aspiration** (intentional living over regret), **FOMO/community** (join the clarity movement), **empowerment/relief** (wife's affirmation story for self-doubt), **exclusivity** (bootstrapped success, rapid sell-outs).[3][4][5] Segment emails: Welcome series for sober-curious onboarding, re-engagement for lapsed carts ("Don't miss your pocket shot"), VIP upsells for loyalists.
