# Brand Research: Brez

### 1. BRAND FOUNDATION
**Brēz was founded in 2023 by Aaron Nosbisch, a 31-year-old serial entrepreneur from Western Illinois who started his first business at age 13 selling t-shirts online and later built ventures in e-commerce, cannabis, and wellness, including a CBD brand and an aromatherapy vape line.**[1][3][7] Nosbisch bootstrapped the launch on 4/20/2023 with under $20,000, drawing on past experience after recognizing the need for a reliable, social cannabis beverage format amid market gaps in edibles and inhalation.[1][3][5] The company achieved $1.3 million in first-year revenue, $28-28.9 million in 2024, and $13 million in Q1 2025, becoming profitable within six months while outpacing competitors.[1][2][3][7]

**Mission centers on providing a healthier, alcohol-free alternative for social drinking, promoting clarity, wellness, intentional living, and feel-good experiences without hangovers, anxiety, or traditional weed downsides.**[2][4][5] Core values emphasize connection, curiosity, mindfulness, and innovation, rooted in Nosbisch's personal journey from heavy cannabis use to profitable wellness products; the brand is based in Palm Beach County, Florida, for its grounding vibe.[1][5]

**Unique selling propositions (USPs) include low-dose THC beverages that are fast-acting, easy-to-dose, and infused with Lion’s Mane mushrooms for cognitive and anti-inflammatory benefits, positioned as an intuitive social lubricant outperforming alcohol or other cannabis formats.**[1][4][5]

**Brand voice and tone are approachable, aspirational, and culturally savvy—relatable storytelling from Nosbisch's dropout-to-CEO arc, with an "ancient-future" aesthetic blending timeless design, premium feel, and modern functionality for mindful socializing.**[2][3][5][6]

### 2. PRODUCT PORTFOLIO
**Main categories are low-dose THC-infused functional beverages (tonics), expanded to a 750ml spirit for mixing/sipping, portable shots, and non-cannabis options, all crafted as alcohol alternatives with wellness ingredients.**[1][3][7]

**Hero products are the original low-dose THC tonics infused with Lion’s Mane mushrooms, praised for health benefits like improved cognition and reduced inflammation; best-sellers drive the brand's dominance in the THC beverage sector.**[1][4]

**Price points position Brēz as premium yet accessible (specific figures unavailable in results), emphasizing high-quality, bartender-tested formulations for cocktails neat, on rocks, or mixed, supporting scalability from DTC to retailers like Sprouts.**[3][5][6]

**Key ingredients/features: Low-dose THC for anxiety-free effects, Lion’s Mane mushrooms for cognitive/inflammation support, fast-acting beverage format for precise dosing, and functional elements enabling social, flexible use without alcohol's negatives.**[1][4][5]

### 3. TARGET AUDIENCE
**Primary demographics: Millennials and Gen Z (ages 21-40), urban/suburban wellness enthusiasts, including social drinkers seeking alcohol alternatives; appeals to Nosbisch's peers in e-commerce/cannabis spaces, now mainstream via retail.**[2][5]

**Psychographics and lifestyle: Wellness-focused individuals prioritizing clarity, intentional living, mindfulness, and social connection; anti-alcohol trendsetters who value functional, non-psychoactive highs, premium experiences, and cultural relevance without hangovers or unpredictability.[2][4][5]

**Pain points and desires: Frustration with alcohol's downsides (hangovers, poor cognition), unreliable edibles/inhalation, and lack of social cannabis options; desire for feel-good, predictable elevation that fosters connection, creativity, and health benefits.[1][4][5]

**Purchase motivations: Seeking a superior social lubricant for events, nights out, or relaxation; driven by health upgrades (e.g., Lion’s Mane), ease of use, and brand's rapid success story signaling reliability and trend leadership.[2][5]

### 4. COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE
**Key competitors: Other THC/CBD beverages, cannabis edibles/inhalables, and non-alcoholic functional drinks (e.g., moderation-focused wines like Madre or culture-driven brands); Brēz leads in THC beverage sales.[1][2][6]

| Aspect              | Brēz Differentiation                                                                 | Competitors' Common Traits                  |
|---------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------|
| **Formulation**    | Low-dose THC + Lion’s Mane for fast, predictable, cognitive benefits[1][4]          | Unpredictable edibles, harsh inhalation[5] |
| **Social Fit**     | Bartender-tested spirits/tonics for mixing, portable shots[3][6]                    | Less intuitive for group settings[5]       |
| **Performance**    | $28M+ revenue in Year 1, profitable fast, retail expansion[1][7]                    | Slower scaling, ad restrictions[4]         |
| **Branding**       | "Ancient-future" premium aesthetic, DTC-to-mainstream[2][6]                         | Generic wellness or niche cannabis[6]      |

**Market positioning: Premium leader in functional THC beverages as the go-to alcohol alternative, bridging wellness and socializing with superior dosing, ingredients, and growth trajectory.[2][5]

### 5. EMAIL MARKETING INSIGHTS
**Resonant messaging: "Elevate your night without the crash—Brēz: clarity in every sip," leveraging founder story ("From backyard joints to $28M empire"), social proof ($13M Q1 growth), and FOMO on trend ("Join the alcohol-exit revolution").** [1][2][7]

**Key benefits to highlight: Fast-acting low-dose bliss, Lion’s Mane brain boost, hangover-free socializing, mixable versatility—use bullet-proof subject lines like "Ditch the Hangover, Keep the Vibe."** [1][3][4]

**Objections to address: Dosing fears ("Predictable every time—no edibles roulette"), legality/access ("DTC + Sprouts-ready"), taste ("Bartender-approved cocktails"); counter with testimonials, guarantees, and education on low-dose safety.** [3][5]

**Emotional triggers: Aspiration (premium clarity for intentional lives), belonging (social lubricant for real connections), relief (wellness without regret), excitement (420 roots, rapid success); segment emails by lifestyle (e.g., "Night owls: Pocket shots for on-the-go elevation").** [2][4][5]
