#### How the Offer Works (Mechanism)
Performance agencies typically use **hybrid or pure performance models** where agencies drive high-ticket clients via paid ads, lead gen, or funnels, charging only on results:
- **CPA (Cost-Per-Acquisition)**: Pay per qualified high-ticket lead or booked appointment (e.g., $100-$1,000+ for high-value services).[2][5]
- **Revenue Share**: Agency takes 10-30% of revenue from acquired clients.[1][2][4]
- **Hybrid**: Base retainer ($5K-$35K/month for mid-market) + bonuses for exceeding targets (e.g., $500 per 100 extra leads).[3]
Mechanism aligns agency incentives with client revenue, using tracked metrics like ROAS (3:1 to 8:1) or CAC tied to customer lifetime value (CLV × 0.25-0.35).[2]
