# VSL Script: Client Ascension - Agency Growth System

**Target Length:** 1800-2200 words (12-15 minutes)  
**Style:** Educational

"You're probably feeling overwhelmed by the constant hustle of finding new clients. Maybe you've tried running endless ads, or even hired sales teams, but the results just aren't cutting it.
