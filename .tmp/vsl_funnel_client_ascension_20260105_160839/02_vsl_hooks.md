# Hook Options

## Option 1: Bold Claim + Curiosity

Transform your agency's revenue by 300% in just 90 days with our proven Agency Growth System—what's the secret behind these jaw-dropping results?

**Why this works:** This hook makes a bold claim about revenue growth, which immediately captures attention. The curiosity gap is created by hinting at a 'secret' behind these impressive results, encouraging viewers to learn more.

---

## Option 2: Pattern Interrupt + Question

Are you tired of stagnant growth in your marketing agency? Discover the strategy that skyrocketed our clients' success and why you haven't heard about it yet.

**Why this works:** The pattern interrupt challenges the common feeling of stagnation, immediately resonating with agency owners. The follow-up question piques interest by suggesting there's a new, effective strategy that others are missing out on.

---

## Option 3: Social Proof + Promise

Join the ranks of agency owners who have doubled their revenue—our case studies prove it, and your agency could be next!

**Why this works:** This hook leverages social proof by referencing successful case studies, establishing credibility. The promise of being 'next' in line for success makes the viewer want to be part of the winning crowd.
