# Brand Research: Brez

### 1. BRAND FOUNDATION
**Brez was founded in 2023 by Aaron Nosbisch, a 31-year-old serial entrepreneur from Western Illinois who started his first business at age 13 selling t-shirts online and later built ventures in e-commerce, cannabis, and wellness, including a CBD brand and an aromatherapy vape line.**[1][3][7] Nosbisch bootstrapped Brez with under $20,000, launching on 4/20 after assembling a team of experts, achieving profitability in 6 months, $1.3 million in year-one revenue, $28-29 million in 2024, and $13 million in Q1 2025—outpacing competitors in the THC beverage sector.[1][2][3][7] The brand is based in Palm Beach County, Florida, chosen for its personal significance to Nosbisch, who met his wife there.[5]

**Mission centers on providing a healthier, alcohol-free alternative for social drinking, emphasizing clarity, wellness, intentional living, and functional benefits without alcohol's downsides or traditional cannabis unpredictability.**[2][4][5] Values include connection, curiosity, mindfulness, and innovation, positioning Brez as "everyone's favorite social lubricant"—a fast-acting, easy-to-dose beverage for social settings.[4][5]

**Unique selling propositions (USPs) include low-dose THC infusion with Lion’s Mane mushrooms for cognition and inflammation benefits, precise dosing to avoid edibles' unpredictability or smoking, and versatility (e.g., ready-to-drink, spirits for mixing, portable shots).** [1][3][4][5] Brez targets the shift from alcohol to functional beverages, blending cannabis with wellness for anxiety-free enjoyment.[2][5]

**Brand voice and tone are approachable, aspirational, and culturally rooted—energetic yet mindful, with an "ancient-future" design aesthetic that feels timeless and premium, evoking social rituals without hangovers.**[5][6]

### 2. PRODUCT PORTFOLIO
**Main categories are low-dose THC-infused beverages (tonics, spirits, portable shots) and expanding non-cannabis functional drinks, designed as alcohol alternatives for sipping neat, mixing, or on-the-go.**[1][3][7]

**Hero products include the original low-dose cannabis tonic with Lion’s Mane mushrooms, a 750ml spirit for cocktails (developed with mixologists like Lynette Marrero), and pocket-sized shots for flexibility at events or restaurants.**[1][3][4][6] Best-sellers drive the core THC beverage line, fueling rapid scaling.[1][2]

**Price points position Brez as premium yet accessible in the functional beverage space, supporting $28M+ revenue without heavy discounting—exact figures unavailable, but bootstrapped origins suggest DTC-friendly pricing for wellness consumers.**[3][7]

**Key ingredients/features: Low-dose THC for fast-acting, predictable effects; Lion’s Mane mushrooms for cognitive/inflammation benefits; functional formulations for clarity and social lubrication, avoiding alcohol's negatives.**[1][4][5]

### 3. TARGET AUDIENCE
**Primary demographics: Millennials and Gen Z (20s-30s), urban/suburban wellness enthusiasts, including social drinkers seeking alcohol alternatives—aligned with Nosbisch's own background and the sober-curious trend.**[2][5][6]

**Psychographics and lifestyle: Intentional livers prioritizing clarity, wellness, and social connection over hangovers; cannabis-curious but anti-smoking/edibles due to unpredictability; value culture, community, and premium functionality (e.g., mixology fans, event-goers).**[2][4][5][6]

**Pain points and desires: Alcohol's downsides (hangovers, inflammation); unreliable cannabis formats (edibles too strong, smoking unhealthy); desire for intuitive, social, anxiety-free highs with health boosts like better cognition.**[1][4][5]

**Purchase motivations: Social enjoyment without regret, precise dosing for confidence, wellness integration (e.g., Lion’s Mane), and cultural shift to non-alcoholic rituals—driven by FOMO on the alcohol-alternative movement.**[2][5]

### 4. COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE
**Key competitors in THC/hemp beverages and alcohol alternatives include emerging functional drink brands (e.g., implied THC peers Brez outpaces) and non-cannabis players like Madre (culture-first non-alc), Kin Euphorics, or pregnancy-inspired wines—Brez leads in cannabis beverage revenue/speed.**[1][2][6]

**Differentiation factors: Superior scaling ($28M year one), Lion’s Mane + low-dose THC combo for functional edge, bartender-tested mixability, DTC-first model overcoming cannabis ad barriers, and "social lubricant" branding vs. generic edibles.**[1][3][4][6]

**Market positioning: Premium leader in cannabis beverages as the go-to alcohol replacement—fastest-growing, mainstream-retail ready (e.g., Sprouts), blending ancient social rituals with future wellness for sober-curious mainstream adoption.**[1][2][5]

### 5. EMAIL MARKETING INSIGHTS
**Resonant messaging: "Elevate your night without the hangover—Brez: clarity in every sip," "Ditch the downsides, keep the vibe," or "Your new social ritual: low-dose bliss with Lion’s Mane focus." Lean into founder story (self-made dropout to $28M CEO) for relatability and "420 launch legend" nostalgia.**[1][2][4][7]

**Key benefits to highlight: Fast-acting precision dosing, cognitive/inflammation support from Lion’s Mane, mixology versatility (tonics to shots), $28M-proof popularity, alcohol-free clarity for better mornings and connections.**[1][3][4][5]

**Objections to address: Cannabis stigma ("Low-dose, anxiety-free—not your edibles nightmare"); legality/dosing fears ("Predictable, social format—tested by pros"); taste skepticism ("Bartender-crafted for cocktails"); ad restrictions ("DTC direct from us, event-ready"). Use social proof: "Outpacing competitors, in Sprouts now."**[1][3][5]

**Emotional triggers: **Aspirational belonging** ("Join the clarity movement—your friends are"); **relief** ("Social highs without regret"); **empowerment** ("Intentional living starts here"); **FOMO** ("$13M Q1 hot—don't miss the shift"); pair with user stories, wife-inspired perseverance, and limited drops for urgency.**[2][4][5] Segment emails: Welcome series for newbies (education on dosing), re-engagement for lapsed ("Missed clarity? 20% off shots"), VIP for repeat buyers (mixology recipes). A/B test founder voice for authenticity.
