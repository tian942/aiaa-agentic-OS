# APPENDIX A: SERVICE BREAKDOWN

## Scalp Micropigmentation Deep Dive

**What It Is:**
Scalp micropigmentation is a cosmetic tattoo procedure that creates the illusion of fuller hair by depositing pigment into the scalp. Steven's "hyperrealism" technique uses proprietary layering to create depth, dimension, and lifelike detail that replicates real hair follicles.

**Ideal Candidates:**
- Men with male pattern baldness
- Receding hairlines
- Thinning hair
- FUE/FUT scar camouflage
- Alopecia sufferers
- Women with thinning hair

**Process:**
1. Virtual consultation (photos + call)
2. Custom treatment plan + quote
3. Multiple sessions (typically 2-3)
4. Touch-ups as needed (5-8 year mark)

**Why Steven:**
- Created the hyperrealism style
- Personal recipient for 8+ years
- Co-designed the Apollo SMP machine
- Created Modern Color Theory for SMP
- Award-winning (Team Micro 2018, 2019)
- Trains other artists globally

## Lip Blush Deep Dive

**What It Is:**
Semi-permanent makeup procedure that enhances lip color, shape, and symmetry using specialized pigments. Creates a natural "just bitten" look that lasts 2-3 years.

**Ideal Candidates:**
- Women 25+
- Those wanting enhanced natural color
- Busy lifestyles
- Asymmetrical lips
- Pale or uneven lip color

**Why Sculpted Studios:**
- Carla has 12+ years experience
- Known as "one of the top artists in the US"
- Advanced color theory expertise
- Natural-looking results
