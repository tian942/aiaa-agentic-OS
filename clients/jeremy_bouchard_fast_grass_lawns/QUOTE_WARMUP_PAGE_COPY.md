## THE REAL REASON YOUR LAWN HAS NOT RECOVERED

Underneath your grass right now there is a dense compacted layer of old organic material sitting between your grass and the soil. It builds up every year. When it gets thick enough it acts like a barrier.

Fertilizer lands on top of it and stays there. Water sits on the surface. Grass seed never contacts real earth. That is why nothing comes up.

Every treatment you have ever paid for has been landing on top of that barrier. Not going anywhere.

That is called thatch. And it is the reason most lawn restorations fail.

Every company in this area knows it is there. They choose not to remove it because dethatching takes real time and equipment. And more importantly, if they fix the problem, you stop calling them.

Their model runs on you coming back every season. We do not work that way.

---

## THE FAST GRASS ROOT RESTORATION METHOD

This is the exact process, in order, every single time.

**Step 1. Dethatch** — The step every other company skips. We pull the compacted layer of old grass, dead roots, and debris out completely. Every corner. Every edge. Not loosened. Removed.

**Step 2. Surface Preparation** — Once the thatch is out we grade and level the entire surface by hand. Seed needs direct soil contact to germinate. An uneven surface kills that before it starts.

**Step 3. Old Lawn Removal** — Everything dead or beyond saving gets cleared. We do not seed over it. Clean surface. Fresh start.

**Step 4. Plug Aeration** — With the barrier gone, aeration finally does what it was always supposed to. Open channels straight to the root zone.

**Step 5. Fertilizer Application** — Now the fertilizer reaches actual soil. For the first time it goes where your grass needs it.

**Step 6. Grass Seed Application** — Madison Parks Kentucky Bluegrass. 21 day germination peak. Laid down with calibrated spreader equipment. Even coverage across the whole surface.

**Step 7. Straw and Tack Application** — A loose straw layer bonded with natural liquid tack adhesive. Activates with moisture. Locks everything in place during the germination window. Breaks down naturally. No cleanup.

**Step 8. Written Watering Plan and Warranty** — Before we leave you get a written watering plan specific to your lawn. Exactly what to do, when, and how much. And if any part fails to establish we come back and fix it at no cost. No phone tag. No bill.
