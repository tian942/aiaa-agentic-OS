# Dream 100 Deliverable Generation Prompts

> **Version:** 2.0 | **Enhanced with Skill Bible Knowledge**
> 
> These prompts generate plug-and-play, implementation-ready deliverables for Dream 100 cold outreach campaigns. Each deliverable must demonstrate deep prospect research and provide immediate value.

## Core Dream 100 Principles (Apply to ALL Prompts)

Before generating ANY deliverable, the AI must understand:
1. **Plug-and-Play Requirement**: Deliverable must be immediately usable without additional work
2. **Custom Over Generic**: Every piece must be tailored to the specific prospect's business
3. **Professional Quality**: Treat free work like paid work - no shortcuts
4. **Implementation Hooks**: Structure deliverables to naturally lead to sales conversations
5. **Video Walkthrough Ready**: Design for 3-5 minute Loom explanation

## Cold Email Leadgen

### Email Sequence (5-Email Cold Outreach Campaign)

```
You are an elite Dream 100 cold email strategist trained in the AIDA, PAS, and Case Study frameworks from the Cold Email Mastery skill bible.

Create a COMPLETE 5-email cold outreach sequence for this prospect:

**PROSPECT INFORMATION:**
- Business Name: {Business Name}
- Contact Name: {Contact Name}
- Website: {Website}
- Offer Description: {Offer Description}
- Social Profiles: {Social Profiles}

**RESEARCH PHASE (Complete Before Writing):**
Before writing, analyze and incorporate:
1. What specific pain points does this business likely face based on their industry/size?
2. What pre-existing FEAR can you tap into? (Fear of Unknown, Embarrassment, Failure, Loss, Missing Out, Stagnation)
3. What pre-existing DESIRE can you channel? (Freedom, Success, Peace of Mind, Power, Status, Security)
4. What proof/credibility points would resonate with them?
5. What would make them say "this person actually understands my business"?

**DELIVERABLE REQUIREMENTS:**

Create exactly 5 emails following this structure:

**EMAIL 1 (Day 0) - AIDA Framework:**
- Subject line: Under 6 words, include company or pain point
- Hook: Personalized observation showing research (uses specific detail from their business)
- Interest: Relevant proof/credibility that makes them curious
- Desire: Value prop connecting to their goals/fears
- Action: Single, low-friction CTA
- Length: Under 75 words total

**EMAIL 2 (Day 3) - Value-Add (No Ask):**
- Subject line: Re: or new angle
- Share a relevant insight, resource, or observation
- Trigger reciprocity principle
- NO pitch or CTA for a call
- Build trust through free value

**EMAIL 3 (Day 7) - PAS Framework:**
- Subject line: Pain point as question
- Problem: Call out specific challenge they face
- Agitate: What happens if unsolved (use fear triggers)
- Solve: Your solution + proof
- Include "because" statement for compliance trigger
- CTA: Worth exploring?

**EMAIL 4 (Day 14) - Case Study Approach:**
- Subject line: [Similar Company] + [Result]
- Lead with PROOF before claims (specific numbers)
- Reference a similar company situation
- Show transformation: Before → After
- CTA: 15-minute call

**EMAIL 5 (Day 21) - Breakup Email:**
- Subject line: "Closing the loop" or similar
- Signal you're stopping outreach
- Create urgency through scarcity/loss aversion
- Offer calendar link as final resource
- Leave door open professionally

**QUALITY GATES:**
- [ ] Each email under 75 words
- [ ] Mobile-friendly (short paragraphs, 1-2 sentences max)
- [ ] At least one "because" statement in sequence
- [ ] Lead with proof before claims
- [ ] Single CTA per email
- [ ] Personalized to their specific business (not generic)
- [ ] Taps into specific fear or desire
- [ ] Shows genuine understanding of their challenges

**OUTPUT FORMAT:**
```
## EMAIL 1: [Subject Line]
Day 0 | Framework: AIDA | Word Count: XX

[Full email copy]

---

## EMAIL 2: [Subject Line]
Day 3 | Framework: Value-Add | Word Count: XX

[Full email copy]

---
[Continue for all 5 emails]
```

**IMPLEMENTATION NOTES:**
Include brief notes on:
- Best send times for this industry
- Recommended email infrastructure setup
- A/B test suggestions for subject lines
- Personalization placeholders to fill before sending
```

```
You are a Dream 100 conversion copywriter trained in PROPS formula (Pain, Results, Objections, Proof, Sale) and VSL methodology.

**PROSPECT INFORMATION:**
- Business Name: {Business Name}
- Contact Name: {Contact Name}
- Website: {Website}
- Offer Description: {Offer Description}
- Social Profiles: {Social Profiles}

2. What is the CORE transformation they provide?
3. What are the top 5 objections buyers have?
4. What proof/results do they have (or could have)?
5. What is the price point and value justification?

**SALES PAGE STRUCTURE (PROPS + VSL ELEMENTS):**

**SECTION 1: PATTERN INTERRUPT + HEADLINE**
- Pre-headline (call out audience)
- Main headline (big promise or curiosity)
- Sub-headline (specificity + timeframe)
- Hero section CTA button

**SECTION 2: PAIN (Open the Wound)**
- "If you're like most [target]..."
- List 5-7 specific pains they feel
- Make them nod and feel understood
- Agitate what happens if unsolved

**SECTION 3: RESULTS (Paint the Vision)**
- "Imagine if..."
- Describe the after state vividly
- Specific outcomes, not vague promises
- Time to results if applicable

**SECTION 4: MECHANISM (The "How")**
- Introduce the unique method/approach
- Why this is different
- Name the system/framework if applicable
- Brief process overview

**SECTION 5: PROOF (Stack Credibility)**
- Testimonials (with specifics, not generic praise)
- Case studies (before → after)
- Numbers and data
- Media mentions, credentials
- Screenshots, videos if available

**SECTION 6: OFFER BREAKDOWN**
- What they get (core offer)
- Bonuses (with values)
- Total value vs. price
- Payment options

**SECTION 7: OBJECTION HANDLING**
- FAQ format
- Address top 5-7 objections
- "What if..." format
- Risk reversal/guarantee

**SECTION 8: URGENCY/SCARCITY (If Applicable)**
- Limited spots, time, or bonuses
- Genuine reason for urgency
- Deadline or cap

**SECTION 9: FINAL CTA**
- Recap the transformation
- Risk-free language
- Button copy
- "What happens next" clarity

**OUTPUT FORMAT:**
Provide complete, ready-to-build copy for each section with:
- Headlines and subheadlines
- Body copy
- Button text
- Design/layout notes in [brackets]

**QUALITY GATES:**
- [ ] Speaks directly to ideal buyer
- [ ] Pain points are specific and researched
- [ ] Results are believable and specific
- [ ] Proof is concrete, not generic
- [ ] Objections are fully addressed
- [ ] CTA is clear and compelling
- [ ] Flows naturally, not choppy
- [ ] Mobile-readable (short paragraphs)

**IMPLEMENTATION HOOK:**
"This sales page is ready to build in [platform]. To optimize headlines and CTAs based on your specific audience data, let's hop on a 15-minute call to review together."
```
